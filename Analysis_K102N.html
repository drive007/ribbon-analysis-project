Key Benefits

  • Maximum Potential Cost Reduction: Offers the highest estimated savings of approximately 586,700 THB annually (~70.8%) among the options analyzed.
  • Streamlined Procurement: Simplifies ordering processes.
  • Potential Future Bulk Discounts.

Risks & Validation Needed

  • Ink Type Mismatch (Durability): (Critical Risk)
    • K102N is a standard Wax ribbon. It fundamentally lacks the smudge, scratch, and chemical resistance of the Wax/Resin (SATO T102E) currently used for critical applications (8-394: JP PACK, Gowns; 8-392: SPT, OEM labels).
    • It is highly unlikely to be suitable for these applications without significant compromise on label durability. Rigorous testing is required, and acceptance of lower performance standards would be necessary.
  • Performance vs. Existing Wax:
    • Must confirm K102N's print quality and durability are equivalent or superior to the current BEA YI B220N (Wax) on its specific materials (Wraps, Masks - 8-393). K102N is a standard wax, while K103N is premium, so performance might differ.
  • Dimension Mismatch: (Medium Risk)
    • Consider optimizing size for suitability and potentially reducing price for 110mm applications (8-393). Requires validation.
    • Consider optimizing size for suitability and potentially reducing price for 85mm applications (8-392). Requires validation.
  • Settings Adjustment: K102N likely requires different printer heat/speed settings compared to current Wax/Resin and possibly the current Wax ribbon. Needs testing and calibration.
  • Supplier Reliability: Ensure consistent supply and quality control from KURZ.

Conclusion & Recommendation

Consolidating to KURZ K102N (117mm Wax Standard) offers the highest potential cost savings (~70.8%). However, like other Wax options, it carries a critical risk regarding durability for applications currently needing Wax/Resin performance, and requires size optimization for non-117mm uses.

Recommendation: The approach should be similar to other Wax ribbon considerations, focusing first on less demanding applications:

  • Primary Focus: Test K102N (117mm and potentially 110mm variant if cost is similar) primarily as a replacement for the current Wax ribbon (8-393 BEA YI B220N). Compare its performance directly against K103N and T102F for this specific application.
  • Secondary Focus (High Risk): Only test K102N on current Wax/Resin applications (JP PACK, Gowns, SPT, OEM) if a significant reduction in durability is explicitly acceptable. Given it's a standard Wax, this is less likely than even a premium Wax.
While the savings are attractive, the high durability risk for a large portion of our ribbon usage makes full consolidation with K102N highly challenging without compromising product quality requirements.
